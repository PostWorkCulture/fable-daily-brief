This Week’s Signal

Two live deadlines land this weekend. The Cabinet Office’s £100k–£163k “AI and Innovation Director” role — a persuasion-and-adoption brief reporting into the Future Civil Service programme, no engineering required — closes tomorrow, Monday 13 July. And GovAI’s Winter Fellowship (both the Applied and Research tracks) closes tonight, Sunday 12 July at 23:59 BST — a fast, low-commitment on-ramp into AI governance.

The wider signal remains that the public sector’s AI hiring is concentrated in adoption, governance, assurance and policy — roles about helping people use AI well, not writing the code. That plays directly to non-technical strengths.
